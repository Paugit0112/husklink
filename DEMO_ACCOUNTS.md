## Role-Based Views

### 🌾 Farmer Views

**Home** - Platform analytics and statistics
- View sold husks in Caraga region
- Interactive map showing market activity

**Sell Husks** - Create new listings
- Upload husk pile images
- AI-powered weight and quality estimation
- Create listing with suggested pricing

**My Listings** - Manage your listings
- View active listings with bid counts
- Track sold listings
- Monitor expired listings
- See highest bids on active listings

**Notifications:**
- New bid received alerts
- Bid accepted/rejected updates
- Payment pending notifications

---

### 🏭 Buyer Views

**Home** - Platform analytics and statistics
- View sold husks in Caraga region
- Interactive map showing market activity

**Map View** - Browse listings on interactive map
- Filter by logistics type (farm pickup / road hauled)
- Click/hover markers to preview listings
- View full listing details
- Place bids directly from map view

**All Listings** - Grid view of all available husks
- Search and filter listings
- View listing cards with details
- Place bids on listings

**My Bids** - Track your bidding activity
- View pending bids
- See accepted bids
- Monitor rejected bids
- Bid status tracking

**Notifications:**
- New listing alerts (location-based)
- Bid status updates
- Seller responses

---

## How Login Detection Works

The system automatically detects your role based on your email:
- Emails containing "farmer" → Farmer role
- All other emails → Buyer role

## All Locations are in Caraga Region

All listings, map markers, and sold data are centered around **Caraga Region** in the Philippines:

**Provinces covered:**
- Agusan del Norte (center: Butuan City)
- Agusan del Sur
- Surigao del Norte
- Surigao del Sur
- Dinagat Islands

**Map center coordinates:** 8.9475°N, 125.5406°E (Butuan City)
